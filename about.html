About Us

Who We Are

Garuda Mosquito Mesh is a locally operated service focused on providing reliable mosquito mesh solutions for homes and commercial spaces. We understand the everyday problems caused by insects and aim to offer practical, long-lasting solutions that fit seamlessly into your living space.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the comfort and safety of your home by providing high-quality mosquito mesh products and services. We are committed to customer satisfaction, ensuring that every installation meets our stringent quality standards.

What We Do

We specialize in supplying and installing mosquito mesh systems including fiber mesh, aluminium mesh doors and windows, and pleated mesh solutions. All our products are customized based on exact measurements to ensure proper fitting, durability, and ease of use.

How We Work

Our approach is simple and transparent. We begin with understanding your requirements, take accurate measurements, suggest suitable mesh options, and complete the installation with care and cleanliness. We focus on doing the job right the first time.

Our Values

  • Quality workmanship and durable materials
  • Clear communication and honest guidance
  • Respect for your home and time
  • Reliable after-service support

Whether you are looking for a temporary solution or a long-term installation, we are here to help you choose the right mosquito mesh for your needs.
